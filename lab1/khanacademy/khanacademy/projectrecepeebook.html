Spaghetti Bolognese

Time: 45 minutes

Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef - 500g
  • Onion - 1 large
  • Garlic - 3 cloves
  • Tomato Sauce - 400g
  • Spaghetti - 200g

Steps:

  1. Sauté onion and garlic in oil.
  2. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Let it simmer.
  4. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  5. Serve the sauce over the spaghetti.

Chocolate Cake

Time: 1 hour

Serves: 8

Ingredients:

  • Flour - 200g
  • Cocoa Powder - 50g
  • Butter - 100g
  • Sugar - 150g
  • Eggs - 2

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Pour into a cake pan and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  4. Let the cake cool and serve.
